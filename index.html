Privacy Policy for Taskly

Last Updated: December 17, 2024

Introduction

Taskly ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Taskly mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

Taskly is designed with privacy in mind. We collect and store the following information locally on your device only:

  • Task Data: Task titles, descriptions, start dates, end dates, and times that you create
  • Progress Data: Information about completed and missed tasks for daily progress tracking
  • App Preferences: Your selected alarm sound preferences and theme settings
Important: All data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any data on external servers.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide task management functionality
  • To send you alarm notifications and reminders for your tasks
  • To track your daily progress and generate reports
  • To remember your app preferences (theme, alarm sounds)

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences system. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We do not have access to your data
  • Your data is protected by your device's security measures
  • No data is transmitted over the internet

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties. Since all data is stored locally on your device, we have no access to it and cannot share it.

Permissions

Taskly requires the following permissions to function:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Required to send you alarm notifications and task reminders
  • WAKE_LOCK: Allows alarms to wake your device from sleep mode
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM: Required for scheduling exact alarm times (Android 12+)
  • USE_EXACT_ALARM: Declarative permission for exact alarm functionality

These permissions are used exclusively for alarm and notification functionality. We do not use these permissions for any other purpose.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can delete individual tasks at any time
  • You can clear all app data through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data
  • You can revoke notification permissions at any time through device settings

Children's Privacy

Taskly is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
